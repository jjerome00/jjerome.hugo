</style> I made my fridge into a TARDIS! I know that you can buy kits to do this, but I decided to try to make one. I used packing paper, some paint, and lots of measuring.
<br />
<br />Colors:
<ul>
  <li>Blue: Empire Fleet Blue, Flat (from Lowe's)</li>
  <li>Black: generic poster paint, Flat (I'd suggest not using poster paint - it's water soluble </li>
  <li>White: semigloss White</li>
</ul>
<br />Fonts:
<ul>
  <li><b>Gill Sans MT</b> (POLICE PUBLIC CALL BOX)</li>
  <li><b>Gill Sans MT Condensed</b> (POLICE PUBLIC CALL BOX (on side))</li>
  <li><b>Times New Roman</b> (POLICE TELEPHONE / FREE / FORE USE OF / PUBLIC)</li>
  <li><b>Gill Sans MT</b> (ADVICE & ASSISTANCE / OBTAINABLE IMMEDIATELY / OFFICER & CARS / RESPOND TO ALL CALLS)</li>
  <li><b>Calibri</b> (PULL TO OPEN)</li>
</ul>
